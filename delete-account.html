Delete Your Account

Last updated: February 15, 2026

If you want to delete your Gamex account and all associated data, you can do it directly from inside the app:

  1. Open Gamex
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Open Settings
  4. Tap "Delete account"
  5. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your game library will be removed
  • Your reviews and stats will be erased
  • This action cannot be undone

Cannot access the app?

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting:

support@slouzilabs.com
