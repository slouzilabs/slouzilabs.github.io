Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Overview

Gamex does not sell your personal data. We only process information required to provide core app features.

2. Data We Collect

  • Email address as the main account identifier.
  • Profile/account metadata required for authentication.
  • Library content you create, including tracked games, ratings, and reviews.
  • Gameplay tracking data such as hours played and personal statistics.

3. How We Use Data

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Save and synchronize your game library.
  • Store reviews, ratings, and user activity.
  • Generate personal gaming statistics inside the app.

4. Google Login (OAuth)

Gamex supports sign-in with Google via OAuth to authenticate users securely. OAuth data is used only for account access and identity verification.

5. Backend Infrastructure

Gamex uses Supabase for authentication, database management, and secure storage of app data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be processed only by service providers necessary to operate the app infrastructure.

7. Contact

For privacy requests or questions: slouzilabs@gmail.com
